Classic Mimosa's

Description

Don't forget to eat, because these will sneak up on you!

Ingredients

  • 3 to 4 oranges, plus more for garnish
  • 1 bottle dry sparkling wine
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt or fine sea salt

Steps

  1. Juice your oranges, either using a juicer, or by hand through a sieve to catch seeds and pulp. Place the fresh orange juice in the refrigerator to chill for at least 30 minutes.
  2. When you’re ready to serve, fill each of five champagne flutes—or wine glasses, or plain old cups—about one-third of the way with fresh orange juice. Add a tiny pinch of salt to each. Carefully open the bottle of sparkling wine, and, pouring slowly against the inside of the flute, fill each flute the rest of the way.
  3. Garnish each drink with a quarter-wheel of orange or a twist.
